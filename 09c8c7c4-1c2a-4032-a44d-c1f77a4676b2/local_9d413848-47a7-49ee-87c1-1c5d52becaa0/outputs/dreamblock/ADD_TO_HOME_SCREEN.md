# Add DREAMBLOCK to Your Home Screen

Make DREAMBLOCK feel like a native app — no App Store required.

---

## iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1. Open the DREAMBLOCK link in **Safari** (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2. Tap the **Share button** at the bottom of the screen
   `(the square with an arrow pointing up ↑)`
3. Scroll down in the share sheet and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Edit the name if you like → tap **"Add"** (top right)
5. The DREAMBLOCK icon appears on your home screen
6. Opening it launches in full-screen mode with no browser UI

> **Note:** Only Safari supports "Add to Home Screen" on iOS.
> If you're in Chrome or Firefox on iPhone, copy the URL and open it in Safari.

---

## Android (Chrome)

1. Open the DREAMBLOCK link in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** in the confirmation dialog
5. The icon appears on your home screen

Alternatively, Chrome may show an **install banner** at the bottom automatically — tap it to install.

> On Samsung Internet, tap the menu → "Add page to" → "Home screen"

---

## Desktop (Chrome / Edge)

1. Open the DREAMBLOCK link in Chrome or Edge
2. Look for the **install icon** (⊕) in the address bar
3. Click it and confirm "Install"
4. DREAMBLOCK opens in its own window, separate from your browser

---

## Sharing with Friends

Tap the **Share** button inside the app (top right of any screen).

- **On mobile**: Uses your phone's native share sheet to send via iMessage, WhatsApp, etc.
- **On desktop**: Copies the link to your clipboard automatically

The link works on any device and any browser.
